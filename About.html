About Us

Our Story

These handmade one of a kind pieces had their humble beginnings in the state of Michoacán, Mexico. The artists who made the pieces are from my family; we have been creating talavera for generations. Now, it is our turn to bring this beautiful art to our community. We have a love for our community and the desire to help these pieces come to life. This talavera is perfect for your home or work place and all of the pieces are dishwasher safe, oven safe, and microwavable.

Our Mission

Our mission is to share the rich cultural heritage of Talavera with the world by creating high-quality, handcrafted pieces that blend tradition and modernity. We aim to support our local artisans in Michoacán, provide sustainable and ethical products, and foster a sense of community through our art. We are dedicated to bringing beauty and functionality into your daily life while preserving the craft for future generations.

Our Team

Our team is a close-knit group of passionate artisans and enthusiasts dedicated to the art of Talavera. Each member of our family brings unique skills and a deep love for the craft. From the experienced hands of our master potters to the innovative ideas of our younger generation, we work together to create pieces that are not only beautiful but also meaningful. We are proud to share our heritage with you and invite you to join us in celebrating the artistry and tradition of Talavera.
